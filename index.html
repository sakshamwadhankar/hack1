About the ISS

The International Space Station is a permanently crewed orbital laboratory ~400 km above Earth, traveling at roughly 28,000 km/h. Partners include NASA, Roscosmos, ESA, JAXA, and CSA. Research spans human health, materials science, Earth observation, and technology demonstrations.
